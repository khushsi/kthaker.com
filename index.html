Driven by a passion for human-centric technology, my research integrates Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to develop adaptive intelligent systems that personalize user experiences. During my Ph.D., I have created systems that dynamically understand and respond to individual users' needs, contexts, and knowledge levels, providing tailored recommendations. I am committed to advancing AI technologies that enhance how people learn, communicate, and make informed decisions, empowering users to stay in control of AI and assist them in decision-making, rather than replacing their judgment or merely offering suggestions.
